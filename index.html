Question
Is this legit?
Yes. We work with US brands to promote their products. You create content, you get paid.
Question
How can I earn money?
You earn by creating and posting content for brands inside Zagged.
Question
How much can I earn per brand?
Each brand pays $150 when you complete the quota. You can stack multiple brands to earn more.
Question
Can I use Android?
Yes, Android works, but iPhone is recommended for a smoother setup and better experience.
Question
Do I need followers? Do I need to post on my personal account?
No. You will create new accounts for each brand you join. No personal account needed.
Question
Is this a full-time job?
No. It's flexible and perfect for students or anyone who wants extra income from home.
Question
Do I need to make any payment?
No. There are no fees to join or start earning. Ever.
